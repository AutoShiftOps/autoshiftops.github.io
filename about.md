    <p>I'm Sajja, an Application Architect at TechMahindra with 13+ years across DevOps, cloud infrastructure (AWS, GCP, Azure), and — more recently — AI-powered operations tooling.</p>

    <p>Most of that time has been spent on the operational side of software: CI/CD pipelines, incident response, observability, and the unglamorous work of keeping production systems running. QueryTuner and the other tools on this site come directly out of that — problems I hit repeatedly at work, built into something reusable.</p>

    <h2>Right now</h2>
    <p>I'm in my final semester of an MBA in Digital Transformation at BITS Pilani, writing my dissertation on AI-powered developer tools with QueryTuner as the live case study. I'm also an IEEE member and write regularly about DevOps, cloud, and AI tooling on this site.</p>

    <h2>What I'm building toward</h2>
    <p>I care about tools that remove friction from real engineering work — not demos, things teams actually keep using. QueryTuner is the clearest expression of that so far: it started as a personal frustration with slow, unreviewed SQL making it to production, and turned into a five-dialect analysis tool with an AI insights layer.</p>
